ABOUT US

We think slightly out of the box, we believe that a club's resources must not only be channeled into conducting events but also to propagate learning and teaching, symbiotically.




AIM

Taking the first step is the most difficult feat to achieve. Let's begin and explore together! Developer Student Clubs is here to help learn and grow as a community by solving and developing innovative solutions. We aim to build an Amazing Development Ecosystem where we bring Developers, Designers and Managers under one roof to inspire thousands of budding developers like you.

VISION

Our vision is to empower students and developers to learn about a wide range of technologies and apply their new learnings to great solutions for real world problems. It is our endevour to be the right mentor for you. Learning is the most important part of DSC, it helps an individual to apply right set of skills and help others to grow ahead in their career. DSC Banasthali Vidyapith is a university-based community group where you work on solving some real problems, develop your coding skills, connect directly with professionals and build a valuable network which would resonate with your goals in life.

MISSION

In DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community. We think slightly out of the box, we believe that a club's resources must not only be channeled into conducting events but also to propagate learning and teaching, symbiotically. We help in solving your queries as you develop your skills and help you grow your kowledge by building innovative solutions.

Community Guidelines



BE NICE
We're all part of the same community, so be friendly, be welcoming, and generally be a nice person. Be someone that other people want to be around. We want the platform to be an excellent experience for everyone regardless of gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disabilities, neurodiversity, physical appearance, body size, ethnicity, nationality, race, age, religion, or other protected category. Treat everyone with respect. Participate while acknowledging that everyone deserves to be here -- and each of us has the right to enjoy our experience without fear of harassment, discrimination, or condescension, whether blatant or via micro-aggressions. BE RESPECTFUL AND CONSTRUCTIVE
We're a very diverse group (100+ countries and counting!), so we're not going to agree all the time. Assume positive intent. The person making you feel uncomfortable may not be aware of what they are doing, and politely bringing their behavior to their attention is encouraged. Remember to be respectful and constructive with your communication to your fellow organizers, harassment is not tolerated*. Don't get into flame wars, don't make personal attacks, don't vent or rant unconstructively. Everyone should take responsibility for the community and take the initiative to diffuse tension and stop a potential flamewar or negative thread as early as possible.
BE COLLABORATIVE
Work together! We can learn a lot from each other. Share knowledge, respond to posts, help each other out.
PARTICIPATE
This is YOUR community, so be a part of it! Join in on discussions, show up for in-person meetings regularly, offer feedback, and offer to help implement that feedback!
